Freelance Hourly Rate Calculator

This freelance hourly rate calculator helps freelancers and remote workers calculate how much they should charge per hour based on their income goals and working hours.

How this calculator works

Enter your desired annual income, the number of weeks you plan to work in a year, and how many hours per week you can bill clients. The calculator will show the hourly rate you need to charge.

Example calculation

If you want to earn $60,000 per year, work 48 weeks, and bill 25 hours per week, your required hourly rate will be calculated automatically.

Why freelancers need an hourly rate calculator

Many freelancers undercharge because they do not account for non-billable time, holidays, or inconsistent work. This calculator helps you set a realistic rate.

Frequently asked questions

Is this calculator country-specific?
No. It works globally because it is based on simple income and time calculations.
