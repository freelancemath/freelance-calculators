Freelance Monthly Income Calculator

How this calculator works

Enter your hourly rate, how many hours you work per week, and how many weeks you work per month. The calculator multiplies these values to estimate your monthly income.

Why freelancers need this calculator

Freelancers often struggle to predict monthly income. This tool helps you plan expenses and set realistic income goals.

Example

If your hourly rate is $25, you work 20 hours per week, and 4 weeks per month, your estimated monthly income is $2,000.

Use this calculator to estimate how much you can earn per month as a freelancer.
